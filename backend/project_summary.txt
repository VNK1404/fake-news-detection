## Project Overview - Fake News Detection Engine

**Root folder:** `d:/fake-news-detection`

### Current Integration Architecture

The v0.9.0 Integration Hardening release stabilizes Phases 1-4:

1. OCR extracts text from uploaded images and PDFs.
2. Text cleaning and validation prepare the extracted content.
3. Claim extraction selects the main claim.
4. RoBERTa performs local ML classification.
5. FAISS retrieves semantically similar past claims.
6. Google Fact Check, NewsAPI, and The Guardian provide external evidence.
7. Source credibility scoring evaluates domains found in claim/API evidence.
8. Explainability builds reasons, evidence, and confidence breakdown.
9. The decision engine computes the final weighted score.
10. Flask returns final JSON for frontend rendering.

| NewsAPI | `fake_news_module/apis/news_api.py` | News coverage and article URLs |

### Artifact Policy

Large model, checkpoint, dataset, and FAISS index artifacts are ignored by
`.gitignore`. Keep production artifacts in external storage or rebuild them
through documented training/indexing steps before release.
